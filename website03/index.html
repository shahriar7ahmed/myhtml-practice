Sahara

The Sahara Desert, located in North Africa, is the largest hot desert in the world, covering an area of approximately 9.2 million square kilometers (3.6 million square miles). Its vast expanse stretches across several countries including Algeria, Chad, Egypt, Libya, Mali, Mauritania, Morocco, Niger, Western Sahara, Sudan, and Tunisia.

The Western Desert, Egypt's vast arid region, showcases stunning landscapes, ancient history, nomadic tribes, unique wildlife, and archaeological treasures.
